Follow these steps to make your contact form functional and receive emails directly.

Step 1: Create Formspree Account

Go to formspree.io and create a free account.

Step 2: Create New Form

After logging in, click "New Form" and choose the "HTML Form" option.

Step 3: Get Your Form ID

Formspree will give you a form URL like:

https://formspree.io/f/your-form-id

Copy the your-form-id part (the last part after /f/).

Step 4: Update Your HTML Form

In your index.html file, find this line:

<form action="https://formspree.io/f/your-form-id" method="POST">

Replace your-form-id with your actual Formspree form ID.

Step 5: Configure Email Settings

In your Formspree dashboard:

  • Go to your form settings
  • Set your email address where you want to receive messages
  • Configure any spam filters if needed
  • Set up email notifications
